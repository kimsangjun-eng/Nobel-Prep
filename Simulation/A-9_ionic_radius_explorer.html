Ionic Radius Explorer

Atoms losing or gaining electrons — and why isoelectronic ions still differ in size.

A-9
Atom → Cation: lose valence e⁻ → # of electrons ↓ → effective nuclear charge (per e⁻) ↑ → radius ↓
Atom → Anion: gain valence e⁻ → # of electrons ↑ → electron–electron repulsion ↑ → radius ↑
Isoelectronic ions: same # of electrons, different # of protons → more protons → Zeff ↑ → radius ↓
