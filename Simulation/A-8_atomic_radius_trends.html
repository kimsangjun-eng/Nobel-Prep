Atomic Radius Trends

A-8
Across a period: # of protons ↑ → effective nuclear charge (Zeff) ↑ → electrons pulled in more tightly → atomic radius ↓
Down a group: # of shells ↑ → shielding ↑ → valence electrons held less tightly → atomic radius ↑
