Gas Diffusion & Graham's Law

NH₃ and HCl diffuse from opposite ends — where do they meet?

G-4
Graham's Law: lighter gas molecules move faster. NH₃ (M = 17.0 g/mol) diffuses ~1.47× faster than HCl (M = 36.5 g/mol). When they meet, they react to form a white NH₄Cl ring — always closer to the HCl end because NH₃ travels farther in the same time.
