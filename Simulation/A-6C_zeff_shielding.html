Zeff & Shielding Effect

Period → Effective Nuclear Charge increases  ·  Group ↓ Shielding increases

A-6C
Effective nuclear charge (Zeff = Z − σ) is the net positive charge a valence electron actually feels. Moving across a period, Z increases while shielding stays nearly constant → Zeff rises. Moving down a group, each new shell adds more shielding → Zeff stays roughly constant despite higher Z.
Across Period 2 — Same Shielding, More Protons
All atoms share the same inner shell (1s²) — only the nucleus grows stronger → Zeff increases
