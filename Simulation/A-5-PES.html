Reading a PES spectrum:
• X-axis (IE, MJ/mol): decreases left→right. Peaks farther left = higher IE = core electrons (harder to remove).
• Y-axis (Relative Intensity): proportional to the number of electrons in that subshell. s-peak height ≈ 2, p-peak ≈ 6, d-peak ≈ 10.
• Gap between peaks: reflects the jump in orbital energy between shells (n=1→2→3…).
• Valence electrons: lowest-IE peaks (rightmost). These determine chemical behavior.
