First Ionization Energy Trends

The tighter the pull, the more energy it takes to remove that first electron.

A-10
Across a period: effective nuclear charge ↑ → electron pulled more strongly → first ionization energy ↑
Down a group: shielding ↑, valence electron farther from nucleus → electron pulled less strongly → first ionization energy ↓
