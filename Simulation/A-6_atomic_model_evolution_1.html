Big Idea

Model Comparison
ModelKey EvidenceLimitation
DaltonLaw of conservation of mass; law of definite proportionsNo internal structure — couldn't explain electricity or electrons
ThomsonCathode ray tube — discovered the electron and its charge/mass ratioNo explanation for where positive charge is concentrated
RutherfordGold foil experiment — most α-particles passed through, a few deflected sharplyClassical physics predicts orbiting electrons should spiral into the nucleus
BohrDiscrete hydrogen emission spectrum (line spectrum, not continuous)Only accurate for hydrogen; no reason given for why orbits are quantized
de BroglieWave–particle duality; electron diffraction (Davisson–Germer)Explains why orbits are quantized, but still assumes a fixed circular path
Quantum MechanicalSchrödinger equation; Heisenberg uncertainty principleGives up exact paths entirely — only probability regions (orbitals)
